The UTM shuttle is a bus service connecting the St. George campus and the UTM campus. In general, students can ride for free with a valid TCard.

## Boarding locations

When traveling from St. George to UTM, the boarding point is typically near Hart House. Students should arrive a few minutes early to queue and present their TCard to the driver when boarding.

When returning from UTM to St. George, the boarding point is usually near the main parking lot on the UTM campus. Exact pickup locations and updates can be found on the official shuttle webpage.

## Schedule

During the academic term, the shuttle runs more frequently on weekdays, with additional trips during morning and afternoon peak hours. Weekend and holiday service may be reduced, so it is best to check the timetable for the specific day before traveling.

## Tips

Allow extra time before classes to catch the shuttle. During peak hours you may need to wait for one or two additional buses.
